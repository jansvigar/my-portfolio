About Me

Based out of San Francisco Bay Area, I am an IT professional/Front-end developer with experience in the Healthcare industry. I taught myself web development using HTML, CSS and Javascript during my position as IT Manager. I was able to quickly pick up these technologies due to my background as an ASP.Net C# programmer. I immediately fell in love with front-end web development. I love how I am able to build beautiful easy-to-use web application accessible by many people around the world. To further deepen my knowledge and mastery of the various front-end technologies, I signed up for Udacity React and Front end Nanodegree programs. I graduated the React nanodegree and am currently working on the front-end nanodegree.

When I am not coding, I enjoy travelling the world with my lovely wife. We are also always looking for good place to eat around the bay area.
